Our Story

Auto Solution is set out to solve the problem of high costs of beautiful and quality cars for those who desire it in Abuja and its environs. We've noticed that most people who want to buy beautiful and elegant cars often settle for less quality ones because of their high costs while those who buy them don't always get the desired satisfaction.

Because we understand the struggles people go through to buy cars, we've put it upon ourselves to give our customers plesant experience by providing beautiful and elegant cars at our car lot in Abuja at prices far cheaper than the amount the same cars are sold in Lagos. Though this is not always easy as it requires that we bear all risks and eliminate the middlemen, we are however committed to providing elegant and quality cars that feet into our customers' budget.

The single most important thing is to make customers happy. A satisfied customer is the best business strategy.

- Micheal Laboeuf

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide beautiful and elegant cars for residents of Abuja and its environs at prices far cheaper than that of Lagos. At Auto Solution, we believe cars could be luxury but most importantly necessity and should therefore be made affordable at best quality for everyone that desires it.

The sold reason a serious business exists is to make life less difficult for the clients.

- Mathew Odgers
